<Summary>
- Introduced iLLuMinaTE, a zero-shot LLM pipeline for delivering actionable feedback to students in online courses.
- Utilizes three stages (causal connection, explanation selection, presentation) based on eight social science theories.
- Evaluated over 21,000 explanations with a "human-in-the-loop" approach using LLM-as-a-judge reliably and safely
- Student preference for iLLuMinaTE explanations was 89.52% compared to traditional methods.
</Summary>
