<Summary>
- Clustering of film characters based on latent characteristics referred to as "personas"
- Investigated the relationship between personas, gender and film genre
- Created networks that map out how characters interact within each movie's storyline
- Analyzed how characters of different genders and roles (main vs. supporting) interact across a wide range of movies
</Summary>


The project was done in team with Yanzi Liu, Ke Li, Mehdi Elbouari and Jiaming Jaing for the course "Applied Data Analysis" of the professor Bob West.
